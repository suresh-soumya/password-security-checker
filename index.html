    Cybersecurity Best Practices

    General Tips to Stay Secure Online

    🛡️

    Enable Two-Factor Auth

    Always add 2FA. Use an authenticator app (Authy, Google Authenticator) — stronger than SMS codes.

    🔑

    Use Strong, Unique Passwords

    Each account needs a 12+ character password with mixed characters. Use Bitwarden or 1Password.

    🔍

    Monitor Breach Databases

    Regularly check haveibeenpwned.com and enable breach alerts to find exposed credentials early.

    🔄

    Rotate High-Value Passwords

    Change passwords for banking, email, and cloud accounts every 6–12 months as a hygiene practice.

    🎣

    Beware of Phishing

    Never click unexpected links in emails or messages. Verify sender addresses — attackers mimic trusted brands.

    📡

    Avoid Public Wi-Fi

    Public networks expose your traffic. Use a VPN, and never log in to sensitive accounts on shared networks.

    🔒

    Encrypt Sensitive Data

    Encrypt files and drives with VeraCrypt, BitLocker, or FileVault to protect data at rest.

    🧠

    Use Passphrases

    4+ random words (e.g. "coral-anvil-sunset-42") are easier to remember and harder to crack than complex short passwords.
